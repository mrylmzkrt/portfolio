The Stamp Collection

Digital Illustration | Personal Project

Type Personal Study / Hobby
Subject Istanbul Landmarks
Year 2025
Tools Used Illustrator, Photoshop

The Concept: Philatelic Architecture

The Stamp Collection is a personal exploration into the intersection of philatelic design and architectural heritage. Born as a hobby project, it reimagines iconic landmarks from Istanbul as minimalist, modern digital stamps.

Utilizing Adobe Illustrator for precise vector drawing and Photoshop for realistic mockup texturing, the series focuses on capturing the essence of diverse structures—ranging from the historical splendor of the Topkapı Palace to the futuristic scale of IGA Airport.

The project aims to simplify complex architectural forms into recognizable graphic icons, maintaining a consistent visual language that honors the traditional aesthetic of postage stamps while embracing a contemporary, multidisciplinary design approach.
