Aura Fraction

3D Motion Study | Abstract Animation

Type Personal Project
Concept Light & Matter Study
Year 2024
Software Blender (Cycles)

The Concept: Inner Radiance

Aura Fraction is a visual exploration of contrast between rigid structures and energetic cores. This 2024 personal study focuses on the physics of disintegration and the leakage of light through physical barriers.

The project depicts a pristine, white metallic shell that undergoes a gradual fracturing process. As the structure breaks apart into thousands of small particles, it reveals a trapped cyan-blue luminescence residing within. This light doesn't just illuminate; it acts as the force pushing the fragments outward, creating a tension between the cold metal and the vibrant energy.

Developed entirely in Blender using Geometry Nodes for the fragmentation and Cycles for the complex light interactions, the animation aims to capture a moment of beautiful destruction—where the shattering of a form is the only way to release its true essence.
