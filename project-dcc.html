Dere Culture Center

Architectural Design | MIM201 Academic Project

Context YTÜ Architecture (MIM201)
Location Kadikoy, Istanbul
Year 2024
Tools Used Revit, Enscape, Photoshop

The Concept: Urban Intersection

Dere Culture Center is a strategic urban intervention located at the vital junction of Söğütlüçeşme Marmaray station. As part of the MIM201 design studio, this project aims to provide a new breathing space for Kadıköy's vibrant and creative soul.

Developed using a complete Revit to Enscape workflow, the design focuses on vertical connectivity and public permeability. The building acts as a bridge between the intense transit hub and the artistic textures of the neighborhood.

The architectural language emphasizes transparency and light, utilizing parametric facade elements to regulate solar gain while maintaining visual connection with the urban flow. It is a social node that invites Kadıköy's residents to gather and create.

2026, Emre Yılmazkart
