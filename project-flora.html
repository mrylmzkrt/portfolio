Flora Living Room

Interior Design | Residential Visualization

Client Private Residence
Location Istanbul, TR
Year 2024
Tools Used 3ds Max, V-Ray, Photoshop

The Concept: Mineral Elegance

The Flora Living Room explores a cold yet sophisticated material palette, harmonizing high-end mineral surfaces with vibrant organic accents. Utilizing 3ds Max and V-Ray, the visualization focuses on the interaction between industrial textures and soft biophilic elements.

A standout feature of the project is the bespoke terrazzo flooring, which provides a seamless and dynamic foundation for the entire space. The multifaceted patterns of the terrazzo are meticulously rendered to capture light reflections, contrasting beautifully with the deep emerald velvet seating that serves as the room's aesthetic anchor.

To maintain a sense of modern luxury without traditional wood warmth, the design relies on the rhythmic placement of plants and the precision of brushed brass hardware. The result is a crisp, architectural sanctuary that feels both grounded by its stone-based surfaces and elevated by its carefully simulated natural lighting.
